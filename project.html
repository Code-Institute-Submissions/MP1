Guys and Dolls

Since October I have been the director for my society Curtain Call Musical Theatre society in our production of ‘Guys and Dolls‘. This process will end on our last night of show which is the 15th of February 2020. I have learnt so much so far during my time of directing, such as, different people need different approaches to explain what you want.

The Vagina Monologues 2020

For the past two years I have partaken in the performance The Vagina Monologues by Eve Ensler in Aberystwyth. After interviewing 200 women she brought together The Vagina Monologues and 24 years later it has been a popular choice to perform in February and March alongside This Girl Can Week and International Women’s Day (March 8th).

Moments of Time

Our second year module we were given the brief on a group project called ‘Moments of Time’, we were to perform this in December 2018 in the Castle Theatre, Aberystwyth. The Brief: Using selected chapters from the text Einstein’s Dreams written by Alan Lightman as a provocation, create a short scenographic ‘poem’ a design as performance event.

Girls Like That

For our musical theatre module, we were tasked with creating a music piece or adapting a current play into a music piece. We chose Girls Like That by Evan Placey into a theatre in education piece mixed with musical theatre. As a part of our proposal I chose the costume designs for the play, we decided to stick to a small budget and keep to a Brechtian style as he was our chosen practitioner.
