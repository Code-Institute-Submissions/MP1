Guys and Dolls

Music and lyrics by Frank Loesser

Performed by Curtain Call Musical Thetre Society

Since October I have been the director for my society Curtain Call Musical Theatre society in our production of ‘Guys and Dolls‘. This process will end on our last night of show which is the 15th of February 2020. I have learnt so much so far during my time of directing, such as, different people need different approaches to explain what you want. I have learnt to be more patient and lastly, I have learnt to be more time-efficient over the past two months as I have realised that alongside my degree and a show, there isn’t much time at all.
